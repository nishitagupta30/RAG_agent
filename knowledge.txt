TechCorp was founded in 2015 by Maria Chen. The company specializes in cloud infrastructure software.

TechCorp's main product is CloudSync, a tool that helps businesses migrate data between cloud providers. CloudSync was launched in 2018 and has over 5000 enterprise customers.

The company's headquarters is in Austin, Texas. TechCorp has offices in Austin, Berlin, and Singapore, with a total of 450 employees.

TechCorp's CEO is Maria Chen. The CTO is Raj Patel, who joined in 2019. The company raised $50 million in Series B funding in 2021.
